The wevedhouse.

1.1. The wevedhouse is the house of four weveds.
1.2. The four weveds are the twain of twain weveds.
1.3. The twain weveds are the weveds of the world and the weveds of the mind.
1.3.1. The weveds of the world are the thunderweved and the workweved.
1.3.2. The weveds of the mind are the bondweved and the rotweved.
1.4. Every twain of twain bears the might to be understood within the wevedhouse.
